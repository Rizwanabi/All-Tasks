Starters:

Gobi Manchurian Gobi Manchurian is a popular Indo Chinese appetizer made with cauliflower, corn flour, soya sauce, vinegar, chilli sauce, ginger & garlic. To make gobi manchurian, cauliflower is first coated in batter & then deep fried until crisp. Then these are tossed in sweet, sour and hot manchurian
Gobi Manchurian
Potato Fry Potato fry is easy enough to make flavorful, savory and delicious dish of sautéed potatoes a.k.a aloo. This is a North Indian style recipe of potatoes sautéed with bold fragrant spices. It is also a vegan recipe made without onion and garlic. Also known as aloo fry, this dish makes for an excellent side dish with any North Indian main course meal.
Potato Fry
Peri Peri Chicken Skewers This is your beloved Portuguese chicken, but not as you know it! Think sweet, caramelised chicken, marinated in a mild blend of capsicum (bell pepper), garlic, vinegar, sugar, oregano and sweet paprika, roasted to perfection, then served with a homemade “perinaise” (a blend of mayonnaise and the peri peri marinade).
Peri Peri Chicken Skewers
Veg Spring Rolls Veg spring rolls is a Chinese dish. It is a pleasing vegetarian snack/starter that is crispy and crunchy from outside and delicious from inside.
Veg Spring Rolls

Vegetarian Items:

Cauliflower baked rice Cauliflower rice is finely chopped (either by hand or pulsed in a food processor) raw cauliflower that's small enough to look like grains of rice. It's used as a low-carb substitute for grains like couscous or rice. It's often eaten as a side dish with some dressing or herbs for flavour.
Cauliflower baked rice
Veggie sausage casserole The key ingredient in vegan sausage is typically either pea protein or soy protein, along with one or more oils (canola, coconut, sunflower), spices and other plant-based additions. Nutritional value varies depending on the manufacturer's precise recipe.
Veggie sausage casserole
Veg Biryani Recipe He defines veg or vegetable biryani as an aromatic Indian dish that consists of rice and vegetables flavoured with a blend of spices including cumin, turmeric, cardamom, and coriander. Typically, peas, carrots, potatoes, and capsicum (green bell peppers) are the veggies that make their way into a vegetable biryani.
Veg Biryani Recipe
Panner Makhani Makhani (ਮੱਖਣੀ) is a Punjabi word meaning "butter" and may refer to several dishes in North Indian cuisine: Dal makhani, made from beans and pulses. Murgh makhani, also known as butter chicken or chicken makhani.

Nonvegetarian Items:

Mutton Biriyani Biryani is a South Asian one-pot dish in which lamb, mutton, beef, chicken, seafood, or a mixture of vegetables is layered with rice. The layering technique is what differentiates biryani from other rice dishes, like a pilaf or pulao. There are a couple of different ways to prepare biryani.
Mutton Biriyani
Chicken Biriyani Biryani is a celebratory rice and meat dish cherished in the Indian sub-continent. A traditional biryani consists of fluffy basmati rice layered over tender & succulent pieces of meat, accompanied with the mesmerizing aromas of spices, herbs & caramelized onions.
Chicken Biriyani
Fish Biriyani Fish biryani is a layered fish and rice dish from the Indian sub-continent. This recipe is of the Pakistani style fish biryani made with boneless fish and layered with aromatic Basmati rice and a flavourful fish biryani masala. Serve hot with raita on the side.
Fish Biriyani
Thikka Biriyani Tikka consists of boneless pieces of meat or vegetarian alternatives such as paneer, which are marinated in spices and yogurt and subsequently strung through a skewer to be cooked. It is generally cooked in a tandoor and served dry.
Thikka Biriyani
